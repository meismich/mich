RANGES for check_snmp command

The options -w and -c defind the warning and critical ranges.

The symbol ':' denotes a range

If the read value falls outside of this range, then warn or crit.

Possible methods of definition:

10	equiv 0:10 means anything -ve or >10 warn
10:	means anything <10 warn
~:10	means anything >10 warn (~ means infinity, -ve or +ve)
10:20	means anything <10 or >20 warn
@10:20	means anything >=10 or <=20
